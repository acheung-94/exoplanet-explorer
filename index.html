This is a briefing from the Interstellar Task Force on Earth. The purpose of this simulation is to ensure readiness for your first deep space mission.

instructions

The program is designed to assist you in acquiring data on exoplanetary systems compiled and archived from NASA's space telescopes and Earth's astronomical observatories and displaying them holographically on your HUD. It will also generate a thermally-colorized simulation of the star system. In the center of your HUD is the rendering area. Objects are colorized based on their thermal signatures.
