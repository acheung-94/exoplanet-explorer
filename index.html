Introduction:

This is a briefing from the Interstellar Task Force on Earth. The purpose of this simulation is to ensure readiness for your first deep space mission.

Instructions

The program is designed to assist you in acquiring data and displaying them holographically on your HUD. It will also generate a thermally-colorized simulation of the star system. Located on your navigation console are the following instruments:

Holographic readout for stellar data.

Status Panel

  • On the left is the status of your long-range scans.
  • Additionally, this is where you may access this entry, should you ever need it again.

Helm Control

  • Press 'Engage' to initiate warp travel to the next star system (the computer will program in the coordinates without your input).
  • Press 'Pause' to pause simulation.
